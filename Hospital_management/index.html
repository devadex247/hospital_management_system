MedOS Scaled Modules

Modular components coordinating hospital staff, patients, inventory, and clinical insights.

Admissions & Ward Booking

Track live bed bookings, manage room and ward transfers, and check active patient hospitalization statuses easily.

Pharmacy & Drug Stock

Auto-replenish indicators, track item batches with safety thresholds, and issue e-prescriptions that links to inventory checks.

Track diagnostic requests with standard LOINC catalogs. Log results and map clinical panels into electronic health histories.
